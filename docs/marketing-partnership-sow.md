## 1. Project Overview

### 1.1 Background

VisionNetworking is an Iraq-based B2B and B2C networking platform that connects clients with suppliers by transforming Bills of Quantities (BOQs) into AI-powered quotations. The platform serves the Iraqi procurement and construction ecosystem, managing relationships across 38 buildings in Iraq, each housing companies with three or more brands. VisionNetworking covers 18 governorates and works with over 100 brands spanning construction, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, lighting, safety, industrial, paint, flooring, doors and windows, steel and metals, and electronics.

### 1.2 Purpose

This Scope of Work defines the terms of a marketing partnership between VisionNetworking ("the Company") and the selected marketing agency ("the Partner"). The objective is to establish VisionNetworking as the leading procurement and supplier-matching platform in Iraq, drive qualified lead generation, and accelerate user adoption across both B2B and B2C segments.

### 1.3 Target Audience

- Construction companies and general contractors
- Project managers and site engineers
- Procurement officers and purchasing departments
- Business owners and decision-makers in construction-adjacent industries
- Suppliers and manufacturers seeking broader market reach within Iraq

### 1.4 Geographic Scope

All 18 governorates of Iraq, with priority focus on Baghdad, Erbil, Basra, Sulaymaniyah, and Najaf as primary markets.

## 2. VisionNetworking Responsibilities

VisionNetworking will provide the following resources and support throughout the partnership:

### 2.1 Platform and Technology

- Full access to the VisionNetworking platform for demonstration, testing, and content creation purposes
- AI-powered quotation system for showcase in marketing materials
- Technical documentation, feature descriptions, and product specifications
- Staging or sandbox environment for the Partner to understand platform functionality

### 2.2 Supplier Network and Data

- Access to the supplier network spanning 38 buildings and 100+ brands for case study and testimonial purposes
- Industry data, market insights, and procurement trends relevant to content creation
- Client success stories and use-case documentation (subject to client consent)

### 2.3 Website and Backend Infrastructure

- Marketing website (Next.js) with CMS access or coordination for content publication
- Backend system (FastAPI) integration support for analytics tracking, lead capture forms, and conversion tracking
- Landing page deployment and hosting
- Technical support for UTM tracking, pixel installation, and analytics integration

### 2.4 Domain Expertise

- Subject matter experts available for interviews, content reviews, and strategy sessions
- Knowledge of Iraqi procurement regulations, industry standards, and market dynamics
- Relationships with industry associations and trade organizations
- Introductions to key clients and suppliers for testimonials and co-marketing opportunities

### 2.5 Approvals and Feedback

- Dedicated point of contact for marketing approvals
- Timely review and feedback on all deliverables (within 3 business days unless otherwise agreed)
- Final approval authority on all public-facing materials

## 3. Marketing Partner Responsibilities

The Partner will be responsible for the following scope of services:

### 3.1 Brand Identity and Visual Assets

- Develop or refine the VisionNetworking brand identity, including logo variations, color palette, typography, and brand guidelines
- Create a comprehensive brand style guide for consistent application across all channels
- Design visual asset templates (social media, presentations, email headers, document templates)
- Produce Arabic and English language versions of all brand assets, ensuring cultural relevance

### 3.2 Content Creation

- Develop a content strategy and editorial calendar aligned with business objectives
- Write website copy, landing page content, and product descriptions in both Arabic and English
- Produce blog posts, industry articles, and thought leadership content (minimum 4 per month)
- Create case studies and client success stories (minimum 1 per month)
- Script and produce video content including platform demos, client testimonials, and explainer videos
- Develop infographics, data visualizations, and downloadable resources (whitepapers, guides)

### 3.3 Social Media Management

- Manage VisionNetworking presence on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and YouTube
- Develop and execute a social media content calendar with daily posting schedules
- Community management: respond to comments, messages, and mentions within established SLAs
- Grow follower base and engagement rates through organic and paid strategies
- Monitor industry conversations and competitor activity on social channels

### 3.4 WhatsApp and Telegram Marketing

- Design and execute messaging campaigns via WhatsApp Business API and Telegram channels
- Build and segment subscriber lists based on industry, geography, and engagement
- Create broadcast content including product updates, industry news, and promotional offers
- Develop automated conversation flows for lead qualification and customer support
- Ensure compliance with messaging platform policies and Iraqi telecommunications regulations

### 3.5 Paid Advertising

- Plan, execute, and optimize paid campaigns across Google Ads (Search, Display, YouTube), Meta (Facebook and Instagram), and relevant local platforms
- Conduct keyword research and audience targeting specific to the Iraqi market
- Create ad creatives (copy, images, video) in Arabic and English
- Manage campaign budgets, bidding strategies, and A/B testing
- Implement retargeting campaigns for website visitors and engaged leads
- Provide detailed performance reports with cost-per-lead and return-on-ad-spend analysis

### 3.6 Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

- Conduct a comprehensive SEO audit of the VisionNetworking website
- Develop and execute an SEO strategy targeting Arabic and English search queries
- Perform keyword research for Iraqi construction and procurement terms
- Optimize on-page elements (meta tags, headers, content structure, internal linking)
- Build a backlink strategy through industry directories, partnerships, and content syndication
- Provide monthly SEO performance reports with ranking, traffic, and conversion data

### 3.7 Lead Generation Campaigns

- Design and execute multi-channel lead generation campaigns targeting both B2B and B2C segments
- Create lead magnets (downloadable guides, procurement tools, industry reports)
- Build and optimize landing pages with conversion-focused design
- Implement lead scoring and qualification frameworks
- Integrate lead capture with VisionNetworking CRM or backend systems
- Nurture leads through email drip campaigns and targeted follow-up sequences

### 3.8 Event Marketing and Trade Shows

- Identify and plan participation in relevant trade shows, expos, and industry events across Iraq
- Design and produce trade show booth materials (banners, brochures, displays)
- Coordinate event logistics, staffing plans, and on-site activations
- Develop pre-event, during-event, and post-event marketing campaigns
- Organize VisionNetworking-branded workshops, seminars, or networking events
- Manage speaker placement opportunities at industry conferences

### 3.9 Print Materials

- Design and produce printed marketing collateral including brochures, flyers, and company profiles
- Create business cards, letterheads, and corporate stationery
- Produce large-format materials for trade shows (banners, posters, backdrops)
- Develop product catalogs or supplier directories in print-ready formats
- Coordinate with local print vendors for production and delivery

### 3.10 Public Relations and Media Outreach

- Develop a PR strategy targeting Iraqi business media, industry publications, and regional outlets
- Write and distribute press releases for product launches, partnerships, and milestones
- Secure media placements, interviews, and feature stories
- Build relationships with journalists, bloggers, and industry influencers in the Iraqi market
- Monitor media coverage and manage brand reputation
- Prepare crisis communication protocols and response templates

### 3.11 Analytics, Reporting, and Optimization

- Set up comprehensive analytics tracking across all marketing channels
- Configure conversion tracking, attribution modeling, and funnel analysis
- Deliver monthly performance reports covering all active campaigns and channels
- Provide data-driven recommendations for budget allocation and strategy adjustments
- Conduct quarterly strategy reviews with detailed ROI analysis

## 5. Timeline

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)

**Objective:** Establish brand identity, build marketing infrastructure, and launch initial campaigns.

- **Month 1 — Discovery and Setup**
  - Conduct brand audit and competitive analysis
  - Finalize brand identity and style guide
  - Set up analytics tracking, ad accounts, and social media profiles
  - Audit existing website for SEO and conversion optimization
  - Develop initial content strategy and editorial calendar
  - Set up WhatsApp Business API and Telegram channels
  - Establish baseline metrics across all channels

- **Month 2 — Content and Channel Launch**
  - Launch social media channels with consistent posting cadence
  - Publish first wave of blog content and thought leadership articles
  - Launch initial Google Ads and Meta paid campaigns
  - Begin SEO implementation (on-page optimization, technical fixes)
  - Produce first set of video content
  - Launch WhatsApp/Telegram broadcast lists with initial subscriber acquisition

- **Month 3 — Campaign Activation**
  - Launch first lead generation campaign
  - Initiate PR outreach and secure first media placements
  - Conduct first A/B tests on ad creatives and landing pages
  - Produce first case study
  - Identify and confirm participation in upcoming trade shows
  - Deliver first monthly performance report with Phase 1 learnings

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 4-6)

**Objective:** Scale successful channels, optimize underperforming ones, and drive measurable lead growth.

- Expand paid advertising budget to top-performing channels and audiences
- Launch retargeting campaigns based on website visitor and engagement data
- Scale content production based on engagement and SEO performance data
- Execute first trade show or industry event participation
- Launch email nurture sequences for captured leads
- Develop and publish downloadable lead magnets (guides, tools, reports)
- Expand WhatsApp/Telegram subscriber base and introduce automated flows
- Conduct mid-partnership strategy review and adjust KPIs if needed
- Begin outreach to industry influencers and potential brand ambassadors
- Test new channels or formats based on audience behavior data

### Phase 3: Optimization and Scale (Months 7-12)

**Objective:** Maximize ROI, deepen market penetration, and establish VisionNetworking as the category leader in Iraqi procurement.

- Optimize all campaigns based on accumulated performance data
- Expand geographic targeting to underserved governorates
- Launch advanced lead scoring and automated qualification workflows
- Increase event marketing presence (host proprietary events or workshops)
- Develop co-marketing initiatives with key suppliers and partners
- Build a content library and resource center on the website
- Implement advanced attribution modeling to understand full customer journey
- Prepare annual marketing strategy and budget recommendation for Year 2
- Conduct comprehensive annual performance review
- Document all processes, playbooks, and institutional knowledge for continuity

## 7. Communication and Reporting

### 7.1 Points of Contact

Each party will designate a primary point of contact and an escalation contact before the engagement begins. All formal communications regarding strategy, approvals, and deliverables will flow through the designated contacts.

### 7.2 Meeting Cadence

| Meeting | Frequency | Attendees | Purpose |
|---------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Kickoff meeting | One-time (Week 1) | Leadership from both parties | Align on goals, timelines, and expectations |
| Weekly status sync | Weekly | Project leads | Review progress, blockers, and upcoming priorities |

- **Ad-hoc Reports:** Available upon request for specific campaigns, events, or initiatives

## 8. Budget Considerations

The marketing budget will be structured across the following categories. Specific amounts will be negotiated separately and documented in a formal contract addendum.

### 8.1 Budget Categories

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Agency Retainer / Management Fee** | Monthly fee for the Partner's strategic, creative, and management services |
| **Paid Media Spend** | Direct advertising spend on Google Ads, Meta Ads, and other paid channels (passed through to platforms) |
| **Content Production** | Video production, photography, graphic design, and copywriting costs beyond retainer scope |
| **Print and Physical Materials** | Design, printing, and delivery of brochures, banners, business cards, and trade show materials |
| **Event and Trade Show Costs** | Booth fees, event sponsorships, travel, and on-site logistics |
| **Technology and Tools** | Marketing automation platforms, SEO tools, social media management tools, analytics platforms |
| **WhatsApp/Telegram Marketing** | WhatsApp Business API fees, messaging platform costs, and subscriber acquisition |
| **PR and Media** | Press release distribution, media placement fees, influencer compensation |
| **Contingency** | Reserve budget (typically 5-10% of total) for opportunistic campaigns or unforeseen needs |

### 8.2 Budget Allocation Principles

- Paid media spend will be allocated based on channel performance data, with flexibility to shift budget toward higher-performing channels
- A minimum test budget will be reserved for experimenting with new channels and formats
- Budget will be reviewed and adjusted quarterly based on performance and market conditions
- All expenditures above an agreed threshold will require prior written approval from VisionNetworking
- The Partner will provide a detailed monthly budget reconciliation report

### 8.3 Payment Terms

Payment terms, invoicing schedules, and currency (USD or IQD) will be specified in the formal contract. Typical structures include:

- Monthly retainer paid in advance
- Media spend invoiced separately with platform-verified reporting
- Production costs invoiced upon delivery and approval
- Event costs invoiced per agreed event budgets

## 9. General Terms

### 9.1 Intellectual Property

All creative assets, content, and materials produced under this partnership will be the property of VisionNetworking upon full payment. The Partner may use completed work in their portfolio with prior written consent.

### 9.2 Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information, business strategies, client data, and financial details shared during the partnership. A separate Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) may be executed as needed.

### 9.3 Term and Termination

- Initial engagement term: 12 months from the effective date
- Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice
- In the event of termination, the Partner will complete all in-progress deliverables and provide a comprehensive handover including all assets, credentials, and documentation
- Post-termination, a 30-day transition period will apply for knowledge transfer

### 9.4 Non-Compete

During the term of this agreement, the Partner agrees not to provide similar services to direct competitors of VisionNetworking in the Iraqi market without prior written consent.

### 9.5 Amendments

Any changes to this Scope of Work must be agreed upon in writing by both parties through a formal change request process.
