## 1. Content Pillars

All content should revolve around these five core pillars. Every piece of content must map back to at least one.

### Pillar 1: Smart Procurement (AI & Technology)

**Core Message:** "Stop wasting time chasing quotes manually. Let AI do the work."

- How VisionNetworking's AI converts BOQs into instant quotations
- The future of procurement technology in Iraq and the Middle East
- Digital transformation in construction and supply chain

**Content Ratio:** 30% of all content

### Pillar 2: Iraq's Building & Construction Ecosystem

**Core Message:** "We know Iraq's market because we are Iraq's market."

- Industry news and trends specific to Iraq's construction sector
- Material price updates and market conditions
- Regulatory changes affecting procurement and construction

**Content Ratio:** 25% of all content

### Pillar 3: Supplier Network & Brand Trust

**Core Message:** "38 buildings. 100+ brands. One platform."

- Supplier spotlights and brand features
- Product comparisons and category guides
- Quality assurance and verification processes

**Content Ratio:** 20% of all content

### Pillar 4: Operational Excellence & Cost Savings

**Core Message:** "Save time, save money, build better."

- ROI stories and cost comparisons (manual vs. AI procurement)
- Process optimization tips for contractors and project managers
- Budget management and financial planning for projects

**Content Ratio:** 15% of all content

### Pillar 5: Community & Iraqi Business Growth

**Core Message:** "Growing Iraq's economy, one project at a time."

- Success stories from Iraqi businesses using the platform
- Local economic development and job creation
- Partnerships, events, and community engagement

**Content Ratio:** 10% of all content

## 3. Social Media Content Plan

### Platform-Specific Strategies

---

### Facebook

**Why:** Largest social platform in Iraq. Primary channel for B2B and B2C reach. Iraqi business owners and contractors are highly active on Facebook.

**Content Types:** Video (short-form), carousel posts, link shares, live Q&A, polls
**Posting Frequency:** 5-7 posts per week
**Language:** Arabic (primary), English (secondary for select posts)
**Tone:** Professional but conversational. Relatable. Use Iraqi dialect in captions where appropriate.

**Sample Posts:**

1. **Carousel Post — "5 Signs Your Procurement Process Is Costing You Money"**
   - 5-slide carousel, each slide one red flag
   - Final slide: CTA to try VisionNetworking free
   - Pillar: Operational Excellence

2. **Video Post — "Watch Us Turn a 50-Item BOQ Into a Quote in 2 Minutes"**
   - Screen recording with voiceover (Arabic)
   - 60-90 seconds, subtitled
   - Pillar: Smart Procurement

3. **Testimonial Post — Client Photo + Quote Card**
   - Photo of a real contractor on-site
   - Overlay quote: "I used to spend 3 days getting quotes. Now it takes 10 minutes."
   - Pillar: Community

4. **Poll Post — "What is your biggest procurement challenge?"**
   - Options: Finding reliable suppliers / Comparing prices / Slow response times / Quality concerns
   - Follow-up comment linking to relevant blog post
   - Pillar: Iraq's Ecosystem

5. **Brand Spotlight — "Supplier of the Week"**
   - Feature one supplier from the network with their story, products, and building location
   - Tag the supplier's page
   - Pillar: Supplier Network
